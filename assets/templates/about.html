You! Everyone from across the world is invited to join this festival by hosting an art show/event in Bushwick, or to attend!
 Bushwick Arts Festival aims to be a world class art event that includes art shows based out of artists' private spaces, gallery events, and other related events taking place within the given boundaries.
A map for all registered events will be available on this website!
All registered events will have free entry. June 3-5 2016. Open studio events are suggested to be open noon-7pm on Saturday and/or Sunday. Arts In Bushwick started a tradition of holding an annual open studios event in the neighborhood 9 years ago.
They have just announced moving their event to October.
Due to strong interest of artists in having a June-based opportunity to share their art with the public, BAF aims to provide a new opportunity for a large scale open studios event. Unlike previous open studios events in the neighborhood, BAF will not exclude artists that do not reside or work in Bushwick. Bushwick, Brooklyn, New York
